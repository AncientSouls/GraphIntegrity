unspreadByPath(pathGraph, pathLink, callbackopt)

Reacts to remove path and reacts to update source or target in path. Then it launched two queues, unspread and spread. This method triggers the first step of the first unspread queue. After removal of unspread from launched from path, you need to start the second queue with method spreadByPath. If passed callback, then not be called removeFromLaunched.
